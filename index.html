HS Hackers Presents

Philly Meetup

May 10 MacAlister Hall, Drexel U

Build

Come to create something awesome, learn how to code, or bring your existing projects. No matter your skill level, anyone is welcome!

Share

Show off your projects with the rest of the community in a massive show n' tell session.

Interact

Connect with local leaders in science, tech, and engineering. Who knows, you may even score an internship!
